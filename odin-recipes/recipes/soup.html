Soup

Description

Feeling down and need a pick me up? Feeling cold and need some warmth? Hungry and need something filling? Then look no more! This recipe takes you through a classic way of making a soup so good you'll want to scream "Eureka!" And the best part? You don't even need a lot of ingredients! Just throw in whatever leftover scarps you have and enjoy the flavor!

Ingredients

  • Vegetable scraps
  • Water
  • Potatoes
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put vegetable scraps into a large stockpot and fill with water
  2. Bring the water to a boil. Once the water is boiling, put it to low and let it simmer for 3 hours
  3. In the meantime, peel and cut up 5 large potatoes
  4. Soak the potatoes in cold water for 5 minutes to remove excess starch
  5. After simmering for 3 hours, strain out the vegetable scraps to leave a clear vegetable stock
  6. Take out the potatoes and place into the vegetable stock and boil for 15 to 20 minutes until the potatoes are tender
  7. Add salt to taste
