Sandwich

Description

This recipe will take you through the amazing world of creating a sandwich. From a toasty bun to fresh, crispy vegetables, this sandwich will make you feel like you've never had a sandwich before. It'll take you back to the days before the tolls and stresses of life came. For those who see food as mere sustenance, this sandwich will make you appreciate food again. Don't we all deserve to experience that goodness again?

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Deli meat
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Spread mayo on both pieces of buns
  2. Take an appropriate amount of deli meat and place on bread
  3. Place lettuce and tomatoes on top of deli meat
  4. Push both pieces of bread together to finish the sandwich
